About Us

Patrakar Mahavikas Sangh: Empowering Media Professionals for a Stronger Democracy

Welcome to Patrakar Mahavikas Sangh, an NGO dedicated to the advancement and empowerment of media personnel in India. Our mission is to enhance the skills, welfare, and rights of journalists, fostering a robust and independent fourth pillar of democracy.

What We Do

Learn More What We Do And Get Involved

Comprehensive Training and Development Programs

At Patrakar Mahavikas Sangh, we understand the dynamic nature of the media landscape. Our Training and Development Programs are designed to equip media professionals with the latest skills and knowledge required to excel in their field. Our management programs cover various aspects of journalism, from digital media to ethical reporting, ensuring that our members stay ahead of the curve.

Excellence in Reporting and Interviewing Dignitaries

Accurate and respectful reporting is the cornerstone of credible journalism. We provide specialized training in Reporting and Interviewing Dignitaries and Public Figures. Our workshops and seminars teach the nuances of engaging with high-profile individuals, ensuring that our journalists can conduct insightful and impactful interviews that inform and inspire the public.

Advocacy for Media Personnel Welfare and Rights

We are committed to the Welfare and Rights of Media Personnel. Our organization advocates for better working conditions, fair wages, and protection from undue pressure or censorship. We strive to create a supportive environment where journalists can perform their duties without fear of retribution, thereby strengthening the integrity of the media.
